Our Story

On a hot July day, Allie strutted into the Superior Dome for the first time. Steve tried to play it cool and ignored her. But that didn't last, and a month later they went on their first date up Sugarloaf Mountain with a bottle of wine and no way to open it. Bada-boom-bada-bang, here they are, 8 years later. They say you'll really get to know someone when you're on a road trip with them, and these two lovebirds have crossed at least a third of the country on 11 separate occasions. Not only that, but he can reach the top shelves, she remembers dog birthdays, and together they can sometimes keep a fire lit after the paper is done burning. It's perfect.
