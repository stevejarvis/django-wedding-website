The Proposal

We were waiting to move into our new house in Aurora, and were surprised one Sunday afternoon when the seller called to say he was moving out early. So we picked up two frozen pizzas, wine, and lawn chairs and went to our new home for dinner! Steve also sneakily but the ring (box and all, it was actually pretty not sneaky) in his pocket. We got engaged that evening in our kitchen, before the pizzas were even done.
